What are Robotic Exoskeletons and what do they do?

Meaning of a robotic exeskeleton:

It is a machine which is attached to the body of the person who wears it and allows for greater strength and endurance. It is powered by a system of electric motors, pneumatics, levers, hydraulics or a combination of technologies. They involve sensors, actuators. The exoskeleton respnds to the body's impulses. If a person could hold 10 kgs before, now they could carry around 30 kgs!
